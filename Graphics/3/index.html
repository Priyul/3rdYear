Instructions: Use the arrow keys to translate the cube in the X and Y directions. Use 'R', 'T', 'Y' to rotate the cube around X, Y, Z axis. Use 'W', 'S', 'A', 'D', 'Q', 'E' to scale the cube. Use '+' and '-' keys to increase and decrease the delta added to the transformations. Press 'Space' to reset all transformations and deltas.
